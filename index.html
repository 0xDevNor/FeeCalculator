Trading Calculator

This tool helps you calculate the break-even price for your trades. Here's how it works:

  • Opening Price: This is the price at which you open your position.
  • Position Size: This is the total amount of money you are putting into this position.
  • Spread: This is the difference between the buy and sell price. It's a cost that you have to cover.
  • Fee: This is the fee charged by the exchange for the transaction.
